# Google Business Profile (GBP) Deep Dive

## Why GBP Matters

Google Business Profile is the single most important local SEO asset. It determines:

- **Local Pack rankings** (top 3 map results in search)
- **Maps visibility** (searches on Google Maps)
- **Knowledge Panel** (right sidebar on branded searches)

**Statistics:**

- 46% of all Google searches have local intent
- 76% of people who search on smartphone for something nearby visit a business within 24 hours
- 28% of local searches result in a purchase

An optimized GBP can drive 30-50% of total organic traffic for local businesses.

## GBP Optimization Checklist (Complete)

### 1. Basic Information (Must-Complete)

#### Business Name

**Rule:** Use exact legal name. Do NOT stuff keywords.

**Correct:**

- "Green Valley Dental"
- "Maria's Hair Salon"

**Incorrect (will get suspended):**

- "Green Valley Dental | Best Dentist in Austin TX"
- "Maria's Hair Salon - Haircuts & Color Downtown"

**Exception:** If keywords are in your legal business name, that's fine. "Austin Emergency Plumbing" is okay if that's your registered DBA.

---

#### Categories

**Primary category:** Most important ranking factor. Be as specific as possible.

**Examples:**

- "Dentist" (generic)
- "Cosmetic Dentist" (specific -- better)
- "Pediatric Dentist" (very specific -- best)

**Secondary categories:** Add up to 9 additional categories. Choose variations of your core service.

**Strategy:**

- Primary = what you're best known for
- Secondary = related services you offer

**Example for multi-service business:**

- Primary: "General Contractor"
- Secondary: "Kitchen Remodeler", "Bathroom Remodeler", "Deck Builder", "Roofing Contractor"

**Common mistake:** Choosing categories you WANT to rank for instead of what you ACTUALLY do. Google uses AI to verify category alignment with your website/reviews. Misaligned categories hurt rankings.

---

#### Service Area vs. Physical Location

**Physical location (storefront):**

- Customers visit your business (restaurant, retail store, office)
- Address is publicly displayed
- Critical: Verify address via Google postcard

**Service area business (SAB):**

- You go to customers (plumber, landscaper, home cleaner)
- Address is hidden (only city shown)
- Define service radius (up to 200km) or specific cities

**Configuration:**

1. If SAB: Hide address in GBP settings
2. Define service area cities (be realistic -- don't claim entire state)
3. Google penalizes fake addresses; use real business location even if hidden

---

#### Phone Number

**Best practices:**

- Use local number (not toll-free 1-800)
- Must match website footer/contact page (NAP consistency)
- Use tracking number ONLY if it forwards to real number (Google calls to verify)

**Why local numbers matter:** Search query "plumber near me" + local area code = ranking boost. Toll-free numbers don't signal geographic relevance.

---

#### Website URL

**Link to:**

- Homepage (if single location)
- Location-specific landing page (if multi-location)

**Example:** National chain with Austin location should link to `https://example.com/locations/austin` NOT `https://example.com`

**Tracking parameters:** Avoid `?utm_source=gbp` -- clean URLs perform better. Use Google's built-in UTM tracking if needed.

---

#### Business Hours

**Complete every field:**

- Regular hours (Monday-Sunday)
- Special hours (holidays)
- "More hours" (pickup, delivery, drive-through, etc.)

**Why it matters:**

- 60% of searchers filter by "Open now"
- Incomplete hours = Google assumes you're closed = lost traffic

**Holiday hours:** Update 2 weeks before major holidays (Christmas, New Year, Thanksgiving). Google uses AI to predict hours; incorrect predictions hurt rankings.

---

#### Business Description

**Limits:**

- 750 characters max
- First 250 characters show in most placements (write front-loaded copy)

**Formula:**

```
[What you do] + [Who you serve] + [Geographic area] + [Differentiator] + [Keywords]
```

**Example (Dentist in Sao Paulo):**

```
Green Valley Dental offers comprehensive dental care for families in Sao Paulo's Pinheiros neighborhood. Our experienced dentists specialize in cosmetic dentistry, teeth whitening, implants, and emergency dental services. We accept all major insurance plans and offer same-day appointments. Serving Sao Paulo patients since 2010 with gentle, affordable dental care. Open evenings and Saturdays for your convenience.
```

**Keywords to include (naturally):**

- Primary service (dentist, plumber, lawyer)
- Geographic area (city, neighborhood)
- Service modifiers (emergency, affordable, experienced)
- Unique selling points (insurance accepted, same-day service)

**Mistakes to avoid:**

- Keyword stuffing ("best dentist, top dentist, #1 dentist")
- ALL CAPS
- Promotional language ("50% off")
- Links or HTML

### 2. Visual Content (Photos & Videos)

**Google's recommendation:** Upload at least 3 photos per week.

**Photo types:**

| Type            | Specs             | Best Practices                                                   |
| --------------- | ----------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Logo**        | 720x720px, square | Transparent background PNG; consistent with website              |
| **Cover Photo** | 1024x576px, 16:9  | Storefront exterior, team photo, or hero image                   |
| **Interior**    | 720x720px min     | Well-lit, professional shots of space                            |
| **Exterior**    | 720x720px min     | Storefront with signage visible; take during daytime             |
| **Team**        | 720x720px min     | Staff photos build trust; name tags visible                      |
| **Products**    | 720x720px min     | High-res, white background or in-use shots                       |
| **At Work**     | 720x720px min     | Service in action (dentist with patient, contractor on job site) |

**EXIF Geotagging:**
Photos with embedded GPS coordinates rank higher in local pack.

**How to geotag:**

1. Take photo with smartphone at business location (iPhone/Android auto-embed GPS)
2. Or use tool like GeoImgr (https://www.geoimgr.com/) to add coordinates
3. Upload to GBP

**Video:**

- 30 seconds max (shorter = higher completion rate)
- Show business in action (tour, service demo, team intro)
- Upload directly to GBP (not YouTube link)

**User-generated photos:** Encourage customers to post photos in reviews. Businesses with user photos get 42% more direction requests and 35% more website clicks.

### 3. Products & Services

**Services section:**
Add every service you offer with detailed descriptions.

**Structure:**

- **Service name:** "Teeth Whitening"
- **Description:** "Professional in-office teeth whitening using Zoom technology. Results in 1 hour. Safe for sensitive teeth. $299 per session."
- **Price:** Optional but recommended (transparency builds trust)

**Why it matters:** Services show as rich results in search. User searches "teeth whitening sao paulo" -> your service listing appears in local pack.

**How many services:** Add 5-20. Too few = limited keyword coverage. Too many = looks spammy.

### 4. Attributes

Attributes are structured data fields Google extracts from your business.

**Categories:**

- **Accessibility:** Wheelchair accessible, accessible parking
- **Amenities:** Free Wi-Fi, restroom, parking
- **Crowd:** LGBTQ+ friendly, family-friendly
- **Payments:** Credit cards, Apple Pay, cash only
- **Planning:** Reservations, appointments required
- **Service options:** Dine-in, takeout, delivery, online appointments

**Best practices:**

- Select ALL that apply (most businesses under-use attributes)
- Attributes are ranking signals -- "wheelchair accessible" helps you rank for accessibility-related searches
- Verify attributes quarterly; Google AI auto-suggests based on user edits

---

### 5. Posts (GBP Updates)

GBP Posts are micro-content (like social media) that appear in your listing.

**Post types:**

- **What's New:** General updates ("New summer menu available")
- **Event:** Upcoming event with date/time
- **Offer:** Promotion with coupon code or discount
- **Product:** Highlight specific product with photo + price

**Best practices:**

- Post 1-2x per week (keeps listing fresh)
- Add photo (posts with images get 40% more clicks)
- Include CTA button ("Book Now", "Learn More", "Sign Up")
- Posts expire after 7 days (Event posts expire on event date)

**SEO benefit:** Posts don't directly affect rankings, but increase engagement (clicks, calls, direction requests) which IS a ranking factor.

---

### 6. Questions & Answers

Users can ask questions on your GBP. Unanswered questions = lost conversions.

**Strategy:**

1. **Seed your own Q&A:** Post FAQs and answer them yourself.
   - "Do you accept insurance?" -> "Yes, we accept all major dental insurance plans."
   - "Do you offer same-day appointments?" -> "Yes, call us before 2pm for same-day availability."
2. **Monitor weekly:** Respond to new questions within 24 hours.
3. **Upvote helpful answers:** Other users can answer; upvote correct ones, downvote spam.

**Common questions to pre-answer:**

- Payment methods accepted
- Parking availability
- Hours on holidays
- Booking process
- Service area coverage
