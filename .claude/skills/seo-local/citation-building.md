# Citation Building

**Citations = mentions of your NAP on other websites.**

Citations are a top 3 local ranking factor. More high-quality citations = higher local pack rankings.

## Tier 1 Citations (Do These First)

**Universal directories** (every business needs these):

1. **Google Business Profile** (most important)
2. **Apple Maps**
3. **Bing Places**
4. **Yelp**
5. **Facebook**

**Data aggregators** (feed 100+ directories):

1. **Neustar Localeze** (https://localeze.com)
2. **Acxiom** (https://acxiom.com)
3. **Foursquare** (https://foursquare.com/business)
4. **Factual** (now part of Foursquare)

Claim these 9 citations first. They power 80% of all directory sites.

## Citation Quality vs. Quantity

**Quality signals:**

- **Domain Authority (DA):** Yelp (DA 94) > RandomDirectory (DA 12)
- **Relevance:** Industry-specific > generic directory
- **Traffic:** Active directories > dead sites with no traffic

**Avoid:**

- Spammy directories with ads and low-quality content
- Paid citation services that submit to 500 directories (quantity over quality = risk)
- Foreign directories if you're local-only (Brazilian business doesn't need UK directories)

**Goal:** 50-100 high-quality citations. Diminishing returns after that.
